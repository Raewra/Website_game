Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Obsidian Horizon ("the Game") is a single-player, offline mobile game developed by Ironveil Studios. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our approach to data collection and usage.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

The Game operates entirely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Require account creation or login
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements
  • Connect to the internet (except for initial download)

3. Local Data Storage

The Game stores data locally on your device for the following purposes:

  • Game Progress: Your mission progress, settings, and saved games are stored locally on your device
  • User Preferences: Graphics settings, control configurations, and audio preferences

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the Game.

4. Permissions

The Game may request the following Android permissions:

  • Storage: To save your game progress and settings locally

We do not request or use any other permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc.).

5. Third-Party Services

The Game does not integrate any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud save services

6. Children's Privacy

The Game does not collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13. Since no data is collected, the Game is safe for all ages from a privacy perspective. However, please review the content rating to determine age-appropriateness of gameplay.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there are no data security risks associated with using the Game. All game data remains on your device under your control.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for you to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your game data, which is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the Game.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the Game, please contact us at:

Email: ironveil_s@outlook.com
Developer: Ironveil Studios

Summary

Simple version: Obsidian Horizon is completely offline and collects zero data. Your game progress is saved only on your device. No tracking, no ads, no data collection of any kind. What happens on your device stays on your device.
